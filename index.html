ND-RVMCC VISION

ND-RVMCC MISSION

ND-RVMCC commits itself to:
1. Develop Ignacian -Marian Leaders who are witnesses of Faith, Excellence, and Service
2. Intensify instruction by pursuing innovative programs that employ relevent educational strategies for technological advancementand stewardship of creation;and
3.Enchance linkages to facilitate instruction, reseach, and community involvement to enchance the quality of life.

ND-RVMCC

The ND-RVMCC Community is committed to become Ignacian/Marian witness of faith, Excellence and Service, especially to the lee-priviledge and to provide a wholistic Christian/Non-Christian Education.

BASIC EDUCATION OBJECTIVES

1. Live as witnesses to Christian and Islam values which find expression in words and actions;
2. Use English and Filipino effectively to express themselves in oral and written communications in school and the outside community;
3. Make Sense of scientific processes and methods to solve real life problems for the common good;
4. Apply intellectually disciplined processe that is unknown using numbers or symbols;
5. Use technology responsibly to keep up with development and research usefull to stewards of creation;
6. Show competency in the arts as self-fulfillment and artistic expression alone or with others;
7. Demonstrate vocational skills on home arts, health, nutrition, and entrepreneurship in service to others and preparation for life beyond the classroom;
8. Manifest appreciation of their Filipinoc cultural heritage and citizenship in order to promote the Ignacian-Marian values; and
9. Exhibit intellectual, spiritual, psycho-social and physical skills in making decisions of life goals, leisure off-campus involment, and in dealing with changes in a developing society.
